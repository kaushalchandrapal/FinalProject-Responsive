Customer Due Diligence (CDD)

As a financial institution, it's crucial to conduct thorough due diligence for every new customer to assess associated risks. This involves verifying the customer's identity, scrutinizing the beneficial ownership of any related entities (even those with complex structures), and screening for politically exposed persons (PEPs), sanctions, and adverse media.
